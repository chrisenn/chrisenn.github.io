About Me

I am a UX Designer who loves to build empathy and solve problems. During my programming major at university, I studied an intro to HCI and I promptly fell in love with the world of user centred design. After two years as a researcher and a ten week intensive UX Design bootcamp, I have been able to sink my teeth into pretty much all aspects of UX from discovery and research through to prototyping and high fidelity UI design.

I like to go beyond surface level and while I am fascinated by all things UX, I have a particular interest in research, usability and universal design.
