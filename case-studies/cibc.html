Banking UXR

Enterprise UX research at CIBC.

Project

My work as a UX Researcher at CIBC is confidential so I cannot show anything specific but I can speak to my process at a high level (happy to elaborate on anything). I have led research on a variety of digital projects across the bank including bill payments, financial goal planning and mortgage renewals.

Process

  • Planning
  • Execution
  • Analysis
  • Reporting

Planning

  • Being involved in project meetings and design reviews to proactively identify decision points and research needs
  • Analyzing existing research and knowledge and identifying where the gaps are
  • Working with stakeholders, designers and cross-functional colleagues to formulate hypotheses and research goals
  • In the case of Discovery Sprints (similar to Google Ventures Design Sprints), this will take the form of a workshop
  • Level setting expectations as to what research can/cannot find out
  • Planning research based on goals and best practices
  • Reviewing plan with the team and incorporating feedback

Execution

  • Running interviews and/or usability testing on UserTesting.com (team members are invited to observe live sessions if they want)
  • Sending out surveys using AlphaHQ
  • Tree Testing using Optimal Workshop
  • Desk/secondary research across the web

Analysis

  • Keeping a rough list of themes throughout execution
  • Reviewing the results of the research in detail
  • Affinity mapping
  • Referring back to the original research goals
  • Listing issues, severity and recommendations

Reporting

  • Presentation of results with discussion
  • Sending out the full deck with links to video clips (if applicable) afterwards
  • Following up with designers for iteration
